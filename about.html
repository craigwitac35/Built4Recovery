About

Built4Recovery is not a finish line.

It’s a standard. A process. A way to stop lying to yourself.

This is for men rebuilding their lives. Not the “perfect morning routine” crowd. The guys who know what rock bottom feels like, and also know what it feels like to get up and still have to carry the weight of what they did.

I’m not writing from the top of the mountain. I’m writing from the climb.

What you’ll find here

  • Recovery talk that is honest and usable
  • Discipline systems that beat motivation
  • Fitness and identity as proof you are changing
  • Simple frameworks, not guru fluff

What this is not

  • Not therapy-speak
  • Not “manifesting” your way out of consequences
  • Not perfection
